Unity games.

Here are some of the games I've worked on based in Unity and using C#!

Little Wanderer is a walking simulator where you are playing as a character that is the size of an ant. Walk around the backyard to explore and discover your environment. The goals of this game is to provide a sense of uncertainty and unknown, yet still allowing the player to explore as they wish.

Flipper's Adventure is a movement based game where you are playing as Flipper, a penguin, who must adventure through an icy landscape. Collect fish, avoid obstacles, and slide on ice as you make it through the game to reach a surprise destination at the very end!
